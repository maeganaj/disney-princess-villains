Disney Princess Villains

by chocobutternut.co

The Evil Queen

Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs


Determined to remain the fairest one
of all, the Queen becomes insanely
jealous of Snow White, the only one
whose beauty surpasses her own.

She disguises herself as an old woman to
tempt Snow White into eating a poisoned apple, in a final attempt to do away
with her only, unknowing rival.

Stepmother

Cinderella


As the cold-hearted stepmother
of Cinderella, Lady Tremaine seeks
to punish and abuse her psychologically.

Motivated by her jealousy of Cinderella
being far more beautiful than her own
awkward daughters, Anastasia and Drizella.

Maleficent

Sleeping Beauty


A malevolent fairy underneath the mask
of stoicism, she unleashes the full brunt
of her wrath with a smile, which shows
how she relishes in the pain of others.

Maleficent curses Princess Aurora to die
by pricking her finger on a spinning
wheel's spindle before the sun sets
on her 16th birthday.

Ursula

The Little Mermaid


Being banished and exiled by order of
the King, Ursula's contracts covertly
designed to advance her own ambitions
and cause general misery.

A villainous Cecaelia (half-woman, half-
squid), who vowed to exact vengeance
by harnessing the power of King Triton's
trident and installing herself as ruler
of all the ocean.

Gaston

Beauty and the Beast


Narcissist who sees himself as superior
to everyone around him, Gaston is proud,
boorish, uncultured, greedy, short-
tempered, bad-mannered and sexist

An egotistical hunter who vies for Belle's
hand in marriage and is determined not to
let anyone else win her heart, even if it
means killing her true love.

Jafar

Aladdin


As Royal Vizier of Agrabah,
the imperious Jafar held passionate
disdain for the Sultan.

He dedicated the latter half of his life
to gaining ownership of a magic lamp
containing an all-powerful genie - to that
end, Jafar would control cosmic power,
which he would use to overtake the
throne and the world at large

Governor Ratcliffe

Pocahontas


Blinded by hatred and intolerance, Ratcliffe
sails to the lands of Virginia with sole
purpose of plundering their resources
and getting rich.

He then provokes a war by spreading lies
against the Powhatan tribe.

Shan Yu

Mulan


When the Emperor commissions the Great
Wall to deter the Hun invaders, Shan Yu
views this as as a challenge and declares
war on his majesty, with the intent of
taking over the country.

He us the ruthless leader of the Hun Army,
who are responsible for the mass genocide
and general havoc across China.

The Shadow Man

The Princess and the Frog


Having come from a poor background,
Facilier grew to resent the rich
and properouse.

An evil, smooth-talking witch doctor
who plots to rule New Orleans with help
from his "friends on the other side".

Mother Gothel

Tangled


Indeed was a phenomenal actress and
performer, a dangerous manipulator and
is very intelligent but had to retire
due to her aging.

A wicked crone who posed as a loving,
over-protective mother, kidnapped
Rapunzel, and locked her away in a
secluded tower, where she hoarded her
healing magic to retain youth forever.

Mor'du

Brave


Originally a human, Mor'du wanted
to rule the kingdom he shared with his
three brothers alone, and went to a witch
to gain "the strength of ten men" in order
to take over the land.

He then turned into an feral and
ruthless bear that is feared throughout
King Fergus' kingdom.

The Druun

Born from human malice, The Druun
are said to be a mindless plague that
spreads like a relentless wildfire,
